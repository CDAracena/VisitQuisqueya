Santo Domingo

Visit the New World's first historic destination. A city filled with life 24/7 and historic architecture all around. Plenty of nightlife activities and easily navigable locations.

Santiago

Visit the Dominican Republic's historic Santiago province. Visit the national monument which is rumored to have the remains of Christopher Columbus deep inside its colossal structure. Santiago is more relaxed, but is still very much alive at night. Enjoy the local bars, clubs and local street festivities or get some peace of mind in the nearby countryside.

Punta Cana

The world famous Punta Cana! Well known for its extensive selection of hotels, resorts and beautiful beaches. Come enjoy your stay at any of the resorts we have. Customer service 24/7, live performances, great drinks, food and the nightlife reigns supreme.
